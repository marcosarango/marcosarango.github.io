My Audience

The audience of this page will be for all the people who need information about the weather. This page is for all the public in any part of the world. The only thing that is required are devices with an internet connection.

Person 1: Juan, 25 | My friend

Person 2: Jason, 15 | My brother

Scenarios

1 - For his birthday he wants to do an outdoor activity with all his friends. His birthday is this weekend. He wants to enter the website and know what time it will be on his birthday weekend. knowing what time will he make a decision.

2- On the weekend he is going to play a soccer game, he needs to know if it is going to rain or not.

3- There is a church activity for the young men tomorrow. He goes to his cell phone to know what time it is going to be. He will make the decision to go with one clothes or another.

4- Wake up at 5 in the morning and turn on your mobile to see what time it will be that day

5- Wants to go out to buy food. wants to see the weather to walk or drive.
