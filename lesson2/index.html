Purpose

The main purpose of this page is to provide all the very accurate weather notifications for the Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven area. In addition to this information, it also includes related news. The goal of the website is to keep the community informed by showing local stories that impact lives and by providing a means to announce events and weather.

This page also have the ability to use it on any device we have. We can enter the website through a tablet, mobile or computer.
